## Slide 2: The Core Industry Crisis
**Headline:** The Hidden Cost of Cloud-Dependent Kitchens
**Bullet Points:**
* **The Infrastructure Famine:** Compounding cloud API and server costs drain OEM profit margins over the 10-year lifespan of an appliance.
* **The Fragility of Connectivity:** A micro-drop in Wi-Fi destroys the cooking experience. Kitchens shouldn't require fiber-optic internet.
* **The Kitchen Noise Barrier:** Standard voice assistants go deaf when a blender or range hood is running. They are built for quiet living rooms, not chaotic kitchens.
